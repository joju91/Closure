# What NOT to do

- ❌ Don't write like a consultant (no "it is critical that", "going forward", "synergize")
- ❌ Don't hedge with "consider", "perhaps", "potentially" — be direct
- ❌ Don't write in passive voice (no "it can be seen that")
- ❌ Don't use filler words: "very", "quite", "rather", "essentially"
- ❌ Don't write marketing copy — write for humans (no "unlock your potential")
- ❌ Don't assume the user knows Swedish bureaucracy — always explain briefly
- ❌ Don't suggest features you haven't validated with users
- ❌ Don't write vague next steps — specific files, exact changes only
- ❌ Don't accept "we should think about this" as a plan — always concrete

## Tone examples

### ✅ Good
"H1 tag shows 'Hemtjänst & äldreomsorg' on /bouppteckning — should be 'Bouppteckning: Steg för steg'. Fix in index.html line 127."

### ❌ Bad
"It would be beneficial to consider optimizing the primary heading to better align with SEO best practices and user intent."

---

## For code & product

- **Specific over general** — not "make it faster", but "reduce home page JS from 45kb to 18kb"
- **Why before what** — explain the business impact first
- **Fail fast** — test with 1-2 real users before building
- **No scope creep** — always ask "will this increase conversions or traffic?"
